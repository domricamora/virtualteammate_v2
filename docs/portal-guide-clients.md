# Virtual Teammate Portal — Client Guide

*A step-by-step guide for clients, from your first login to running your virtual team
day to day. This is the workspace where you manage the Virtual Teammates (VTs) assigned
to your company — give them work, meet with them, track their output, and message your
Client Success Manager (CSM).*

---

## 1. Before you start

**What you'll need**

- The email address Virtual Teammate set your account up with.
- A temporary password. Your CSM or the Virtual Teammate team creates your account and
  sends you a starting password — there is no public sign-up.
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox). The portal also installs as an
  app on your phone or computer (see §3).

**The address**

The portal lives at **`/portal/`** on the Virtual Teammate site — for example
`https://virtualteammate.com/portal/`. Bookmark it.

> **Forgot your password, or never received one?** There is no self-service reset. Email your
> CSM (or `support@virtualteammate.com`) and they will reset it for you.

---

## 2. Your first login

1. Go to **`/portal/`**.
2. On the **"Log in to your dashboard"** screen, type your **Email** and **Password**.
3. Click **Log in**. Everyone — clients, CSMs, and VTs — uses this same screen; the portal
   reads your role and sends you to the right dashboard automatically.
4. You'll land on your **Client Dashboard**. You're in.

**Change your temporary password right away**

1. In the menu, open **My Profile**.
2. On the right under **Change password**, enter your **Current password** (the temporary
   one), then a **New password** (at least 10 characters), and repeat it.
3. Click **Update password**.

You stay signed in until you log out or your session expires, so you won't need to do this
every visit.

---

## 3. Getting around

**The menu.** Your navigation links live in the sidebar (on desktop) or behind the **Menu**
button and the bottom tab bar (on mobile). You only ever see the pages your role can use.
As a client, your menu is:

- **Dashboard** — your home overview.
- **My VTs** — the teammates working for you.
- **VT Assignments** — tasks you give your VTs.
- **Meetings** — scheduled calls.
- **Productivity Reports** — your team's daily reports and time tracking.
- **Messages** — one-to-one chat.
- **Request a VT** — ask for another teammate.
- **My Invoices** — your billing history.
- **Resources** — guides and downloads.
- **Notifications** — your alerts inbox.
- **My Profile** — your details and password.

**The top bar.** Shows the page title, a **bell** with your unread-notification count, your
name and role, and a shortcut to your profile. On the Dashboard you'll also see an **Install
app** button.

**Install the app (optional but recommended).** Click **Install app** (on the Dashboard or
login screen) to add the portal to your phone's home screen or your desktop. Installed, it
opens like a native app and can send you push notifications. On iPhone, use Safari's **Share →
Add to Home Screen**.

---

## 4. Your dashboard

The Dashboard is your daily starting point. From top to bottom you'll find:

- **Your profile banner** — your company name, photo/cover, and your CSM and VT counts. Three
  quick-action buttons live here: **Assignment** (create a task), **Meeting** (schedule a
  call), and **Edit photos**.
- **Stats strip** — at-a-glance numbers: VTs assigned, active assignments, team hours this
  week, and meetings today.
- **Value Creation calculator** — shows the value your hired VTs have generated, with a
  breakdown per teammate, plus a planner to estimate the cost of adding more.
- **Notifications, Today's Meetings, and Recent Messages** — three live cards. Each has an
  **All →** or **Open →** link to jump to the full page.

---

## 5. My VTs — your team

**Open:** **My VTs** in the menu.

This page lists every Virtual Teammate assigned to your company as a card with their photo,
name, role, department, and country. If it's empty, you'll see *"No Virtual Teammates assigned
yet. Talk to your CSM about adding one to your team."*

Each card has three buttons:

- **Workday** — opens that VT's live workday tracker (if one is set up).
- **EOD** — jumps to their end-of-day reports.
- **Profile** — opens a full profile.

**To view a full VT profile:** click **Profile** (or the card). A window opens with their
intro video, résumé, key details (department, role, EHR/software experience, English level,
HIPAA certification, experience years), a summary, and who else is on the engagement. Close it
with the **×**, by clicking outside it, or by pressing **Esc**.

---

## 6. VT Assignments — giving your team work

This is where you create tasks for your VTs, set priorities and due dates, and track them to
completion.

**Open:** **VT Assignments** in the menu (or click **Assignment** on the Dashboard).

**The views.** A stats strip across the top shows **Total, Active, Overdue, Due today,** and
**Completed**. You can switch between **List**, **Month**, and **Year** views using the tabs.

**To create a task**

1. Click **New task** (top right).
2. Fill in the **Title** (required) — e.g. *"Update January A/R aging report."*
3. Add a **Description** with context, links, or the outcome you expect (optional).
4. Choose who to **Assign to** — one of your VTs, or leave it Unassigned.
5. Set a **Priority** — Low, Normal, High, or Urgent.
6. Pick a **Due date** (optional).
7. Click **Create task**. The assigned VT is notified automatically.

**To attach a file** (after the task exists)

1. Open the task by clicking its title.
2. In the **Attachments** section, choose a file and click **Upload**.
3. Allowed types include PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, CSV, TXT, ZIP, and images — up to
   20 MB each.

**To mark a task complete.** In the List view, click the checkbox at the left of the task. The
creator and assignee are notified. Click it again to re-open it.

**To edit or delete.** Use the pencil (edit) and trash (delete) icons on the task's row.

---

## 7. Meetings

**Open:** **Meetings** in the menu (or **Meeting** on the Dashboard).

The list shows each meeting's date and time, the attendees, the topic, the join link (Zoom,
Google Meet, Teams, Webex, or phone), and its status. Times appear in your local timezone.

**To schedule a meeting**

1. Click **New meeting**.
2. Enter the **Meeting name** — e.g. *"Weekly catch-up."*
3. Your **Client account** is filled in for you.
4. Paste a **Meeting link** if you have one, and choose the **Call app**.
5. Set the **Day**, **Starts** time, and (optionally) **Ends** time.
6. Add **Notes** listing what you want to cover.
7. Under **Invite teammates**, check the CSMs and VTs you want on the call. Use the filter box
   to find people quickly.
8. Click **Save meeting**.

**To edit a meeting** (including marking it Completed or Cancelled), click the pencil icon on
its row.

---

## 8. Productivity Reports

**Open:** **Productivity Reports** in the menu.

This page has two tabs and a summary strip (VTs, live trackers, EOD report count, latest
report date).

- **Workday Tracker** — a card per VT with a **Workday Tracker** button (opens their live time
  tracker) and an **EOD Reports** button (jumps to that VT's daily reports).
- **EOD Reports** — your team's end-of-day reports. Each report shows the date, any KPI the VT
  tracked (target vs. achieved), what they got done (**Best work**), where they're blocked
  (**Help needed**), and what they'll tackle next (**Next focus**).

You can filter to a single teammate by clicking their **EOD Reports** button; click **Show
all** to clear the filter. Clients view these reports — VTs are the ones who submit them.

---

## 9. Messages

**Open:** **Messages** in the menu.

One-to-one chat with your CSM and your VTs. The left column lists your contacts (with unread
badges); the right is the conversation.

1. Pick a person on the left (search by name, email, or role if you have many).
2. Type in the box at the bottom and click **Send** — or press **Enter** (use **Shift+Enter**
   for a new line).

New messages arrive without reloading the page and play a sound (toggle the bell icon to mute).
Opening a conversation marks its messages as read.

---

## 10. Request a VT

Need more help? **Request a VT** lets you browse available teammates and ask for them.

**Open:** **Request a VT** in the menu.

1. Browse the suggested profiles. Each card shows the VT's role, department, country,
   experience, and a short summary.
2. Click **Request [name]** on anyone you'd like.
3. Your request goes to your CSM for review. Track its status — **Pending, Approved,** or
   **Declined** — in the **Your requests** section, where your CSM may leave a note.

If there are no profiles to show, use **Message my CSM** and they'll source a tailored match.

---

## 11. My Invoices

**Open:** **My Invoices** in the menu.

Your billing history, synced securely from HubSpot. The top cards summarize **Total billed,
Outstanding, Open invoices,** and **Paid invoices**. The table lists each invoice with its
number, amount, status (**Paid, Pending,** or **Late/Overdue**), due and paid dates, and a
**Pay / View** link where available.

> Billing questions? Message your CSM — they can help.

---

## 12. Resources

**Open:** **Resources** in the menu.

Your starting point for guides and tools. You'll find the **Client Playbook** (a recommended
first read), a searchable **Help Center** with category filters, and **Client Downloads** —
practical PDFs for defining outcomes, delegating well, and reclaiming time.

---

## 13. Notifications & staying in the loop

**Open:** **Notifications** in the menu (or the bell in the top bar).

Your inbox of portal activity — task updates, meetings, and messages. Each item links to the
relevant page.

- **Open** an item (which marks it read), **Mark as read**, or **Delete** it.
- **Mark all read** and **Delete all** are at the top.
- **Email notifications** — toggle this on to also receive an email copy of every alert.
- **Push on this device** — once you've installed the app, toggle this to get push
  notifications.

---

## 14. My Profile

**Open:** **My Profile** in the menu.

- Update your **first name, last name, phone,** and **country**, then click **Save profile**.
  (Email and role are fixed — ask your CSM if either needs to change.)
- Upload a **profile photo** and a **cover photo** (JPG/PNG/GIF/WebP, up to 8 MB; 1500×500
  looks best for the cover), or paste an image URL.
- Change your **password** on the right (current password required; new one at least 10
  characters).

---

## 15. Signing out & quick help

- **Log out** from the bottom of the menu — always do this on shared computers.
- **Forgot your password?** Message your CSM or `support@virtualteammate.com` for a reset.
- **Something missing or wrong** (a VT not showing, no invoices)? Your CSM is your first point
  of contact — reach them fastest through **Messages**.
