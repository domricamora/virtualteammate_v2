# Virtual Teammate Portal — Virtual Teammate (VT) Guide

*A step-by-step guide for Virtual Teammates, from your first login to your daily routine. The
portal is where you see your assignments, join meetings, submit your end-of-day reports, chat
with your team, view payslips, and grow with the company. This guide covers both **hired** VTs
(placed with a client) and **on-pool** VTs (ready and waiting for a placement).*

---

## 1. Before you start

**What you'll need**

- The email address your account was created with.
- A temporary password from the Virtual Teammate team (there is no public sign-up).
- A modern browser. The portal also installs as an app on your phone (see §3) — handy for a
  VT who's always on the move.

**The address**

The portal lives at **`/portal/`** on the Virtual Teammate site — e.g.
`https://virtualteammate.com/portal/`. Bookmark it.

> **No password, or forgot it?** There's no self-service reset. Message your CSM or
> `support@virtualteammate.com` and they'll reset it for you.

---

## 2. Your first login

1. Go to **`/portal/`**.
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password** on the **"Log in to your dashboard"** screen and click
   **Log in**. The portal sends you to your VT dashboard automatically.
3. Change your temporary password right away: open **My Profile**, and under **Change
   password** enter your current password, a new one (at least 10 characters), and repeat it.
   Click **Update password**.

You stay signed in until you log out or the session expires.

---

## 3. Getting around

**Your menu depends on your status.**

**Hired VTs** see the full menu:

- **Dashboard** — your home.
- **My Assignments** — tasks from your client/CSM.
- **My Meetings** — your scheduled calls.
- **Productivity Reports** — submit and review your end-of-day (EOD) reports.
- **Messages** — chat with your team.
- **My CSM & Teammates** — who manages you and who you work alongside.
- **Payslips** — your pay history.
- **Resources** — training and SOPs.
- **VTM Apps** — approved tools to install.
- **Refer A Friend** — refer someone and earn.
- **Notifications** — your alerts.
- **My Profile** — your details and password.

**On-pool VTs** (not yet placed) see a focused menu: **Dashboard, Productivity Reports,
Messages, Resources, VTM Apps, Refer A Friend, Notifications,** and **My Profile**. Once you're
placed with a client, the rest — assignments, meetings, payslips, and your CSM & teammates —
appear automatically.

**The top bar** shows the page title, the **bell** with your unread count, your name, and a
profile shortcut. On the Dashboard there's an **Install app** button.

**Install the app (recommended).** Click **Install app** to add the portal to your phone's home
screen so you get push notifications and one-tap access. On iPhone, use Safari's **Share → Add
to Home Screen**.

---

## 4. Your dashboard

Your daily launch pad. Depending on your status you'll see:

- **Your banner** — your photo, role, years of experience, and (if hired) your client and CSM
  count. Quick buttons: **New EOD**, **Workday Tracker** (opens your time tracker), and **Edit
  photos**.
- **My profile** — a read-only summary: department, role, experience, software/EHR, English
  level, status.
- **My client** and **My CSM** (hired VTs) — your placement and who manages you.
- **Recent EOD reports** — your latest daily reports, with a **New EOD report** button.

On-pool VTs see the same dashboard minus the client and CSM cards.

---

## 5. My Assignments — your tasks *(hired VTs)*

**Open:** **My Assignments** in the menu.

Every task assigned to you, with a stats strip (**Total, Active, Overdue, Due today,
Completed**) and **List / Month / Year** views.

Each task shows its title, priority (Low/Normal/High/Urgent), due date (red if overdue), and
status. To see the full details, links, and any attachments, click the task title.

**To mark a task complete**

1. Find the task in the **List** view.
2. Click the **checkbox** at the left of its row. The status flips to **Completed** and your
   client and CSM are notified.
3. Click it again to **re-open** the task if needed.

> You can update and complete the tasks assigned to you, but new tasks are created by your
> client or CSM.

---

## 6. My Meetings *(hired VTs)*

**Open:** **My Meetings** in the menu.

Your scheduled calls, each showing the date and time, the client, the other attendees, the
topic, and the join link (Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, Webex, or phone). Times are in your local
timezone. Click a meeting's join link when it's time to connect.

---

## 7. Productivity Reports & your daily EOD *(core daily habit)*

**Open:** **Productivity Reports** in the menu.

Your end-of-day (EOD) report is the heartbeat of your day — it keeps your client and CSM in the
loop on what you accomplished and where you need help. The page has a **Workday Tracker** tab
(quick access to your time tracker) and an **EOD Reports** tab (submit and review reports).

**To submit an EOD report**

1. On the **EOD Reports** tab, click **+ New report** (or **New EOD** from your dashboard).
2. Set the **Date** (defaults to today).
3. Optionally record a KPI: **KPI name** (e.g. *Calls handled*), **KPI target** (e.g. *40*),
   and **KPI achieved** (e.g. *43*).
4. Fill in the short prompts:
5. **Best work today** — what went well or what you shipped.
6. **Help needed** — anything blocking you.
7. **Focus next** — what you'll tackle next.
8. **Pending / waiting on** — items waiting on someone else.
9. Click **Submit EOD report**. It appears in your history below, and your client and CSM can
   read it.

You can expand any past report in the history list to review what you wrote. Aim to submit one
at the end of every working day.

> **On-pool VTs:** you can see this page, but you'll start logging EOD reports once you're
> placed with a client.

**Logging your hours.** Use the **Workday Tracker** button (on the dashboard or the Workday
Tracker tab) to open your time tracker and log your working hours.

---

## 8. Messages

**Open:** **Messages** in the menu.

One-to-one chat with the people on your team — your CSM, your client, and your fellow VTs.

1. Pick a contact on the left (search by name, email, or role).
2. Type at the bottom and click **Send** — or press **Enter** (**Shift+Enter** for a new line).

New messages arrive without reloading and play a sound (mute it with the bell icon). Opening a
conversation marks its messages as read.

---

## 9. My CSM & Teammates *(hired VTs)*

**Open:** **My CSM & Teammates** in the menu.

Your engagement at a glance: your **client** contact, your **CSM(s)**, and the **teammates** on
the same account. Each person card has **Chat**, **Email**, and **Call** buttons so you can
reach anyone quickly. Before you're placed you'll see *"No active engagement yet"* — it fills
in once you join a client.

---

## 10. Payslips *(hired VTs)*

**Open:** **Payslips** in the menu.

Your pay history, synced from payroll. The top cards show how many records are available, your
latest pay period, and your tracked payout total. The table lists each payslip, newest first.
If you believe something's missing, contact HR.

---

## 11. Resources & VTM Apps

**Resources** — your training and reference library: a searchable **Help Center** (filter by
**Operations / Admin / Support**) plus standard SOPs (attendance, time off, health benefits,
and role-specific guides). Open any tile to read it.

**VTM Apps** — the directory of approved tools to install (communication, time tracking, and
more). Each card has a button to download or open the app.

---

## 12. Refer A Friend

**Open:** **Refer A Friend** in the menu.

Know someone who'd be a great Virtual Teammate? Refer them:

1. Enter your friend's **name** and **email**.
2. Add an optional **note** about them.
3. Click **Send referral**.

---

## 13. Notifications & staying in the loop

**Open:** **Notifications** in the menu (or the top-bar bell).

Your inbox of alerts — new tasks, meetings, and messages — each linking to the right page.

- **Open**, **Mark as read**, or **Delete** items; **Mark all read** and **Delete all** sit at
  the top.
- **Email notifications** — toggle on to also get email copies.
- **Push on this device** — after installing the app, toggle on for push alerts (great for not
  missing a new task or message).

---

## 14. My Profile

**Open:** **My Profile** in the menu.

Update your **name, phone,** and **country** and click **Save profile** (email and role are
fixed — ask your CSM if they need changing). Add a **profile photo** and **cover photo**
(JPG/PNG/GIF/WebP, up to 8 MB), or paste a URL. Change your **password** on the right (10+
characters).

A complete, professional profile helps when clients review who's on their team — keep your
photo and details current.

---

## 15. Signing out & quick help

- **Log out** from the bottom of the menu — always do this on shared computers.
- **Forgot your password?** Message your CSM or `support@virtualteammate.com` for a reset.
- **Not placed yet?** Stay sharp: keep your profile polished, complete your training in
  **Resources**, and check **Messages** and **Notifications** so you don't miss an opportunity.

| I want to… | Go to | Then |
|------------|-------|------|
| See today's tasks | My Assignments | Review the List view |
| Mark a task done | My Assignments | Click the checkbox on its row |
| Submit my end-of-day report | Productivity Reports | EOD Reports → + New report → Submit |
| Log my hours | Productivity Reports / Dashboard | Workday Tracker button |
| Join a call | My Meetings | Click the meeting's join link |
| Message my CSM or client | Messages | Pick the person → type → Send |
| Check my pay | Payslips | Review the ledger |
| Refer a friend | Refer A Friend | Fill in → Send referral |
| Change my password | My Profile | Change password → Update password |
