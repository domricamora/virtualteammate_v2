# Virtual Teammate Portal — CSM Guide

*A step-by-step guide for Client Success Managers (CSMs), from your first login to managing
your book of business day to day. The portal is where you run your client accounts: their
Virtual Teammates (VTs), the work you assign, the meetings you schedule, the requests you
approve, and the relationships you keep healthy.*

---

## 1. Before you start

**What you'll need**

- The email address your account was set up with.
- A temporary password from the Virtual Teammate team (there is no public sign-up).
- A modern browser. The portal also installs as an app (see §3).

**The address**

The portal lives at **`/portal/`** on the Virtual Teammate site — e.g.
`https://virtualteammate.com/portal/`. Bookmark it.

> **No password, or forgot it?** There is no self-service reset. Ask a super admin (Virtual
> Teammate operations) to reset it from the Users page.

---

## 2. Your first login

1. Go to **`/portal/`**.
2. On the **"Log in to your dashboard"** screen, enter your **Email** and **Password** and
   click **Log in**. Everyone uses this one screen; the portal routes you to your **CSM
   Dashboard** based on your role.
3. Change your temporary password immediately: open **My Profile**, and under **Change
   password** enter your current password, a new one (10+ characters), and repeat it, then
   click **Update password**.

You stay signed in until you log out or the session expires.

---

## 3. Getting around

**Your menu.** You only see what your role can use. As a CSM:

- **Dashboard** — your portfolio overview.
- **My Clients & VTs** — your accounts and their teams; approve VT requests here.
- **VT Assignments** — tasks across your clients' VTs.
- **Meetings** — calls with clients and VTs.
- **Productivity Reports** — workday trackers and EOD reports across your book.
- **Messages** — chat with clients and VTs.
- **Special Links** — shareable, no-login VT profile links.
- **Resources** — guides and the Knowledge Center.
- **Notifications** — your alerts inbox.
- **My Profile** — your details and password.

There's also a **Marketing site** shortcut at the bottom of the menu, and **Log out**.

**The top bar.** Page title, the **bell** with your unread count, your name and role, and a
profile shortcut. On the Dashboard you'll see an **Install app** button.

**Install the app (recommended).** Click **Install app** to add the portal to your desktop or
phone home screen so you get push notifications and a native-app feel. On iPhone, use Safari's
**Share → Add to Home Screen**.

---

## 4. Your dashboard

Your home is a portfolio snapshot:

- **Your banner** — your name, photo, and counts of clients, VTs, and recent meetings, with
  quick buttons: **My Clients**, **Schedule meeting**, and **Edit photos**.
- **Activity — last 14 days** — a trend chart of EOD Reports, VT Assignments, and Meetings.
  Hover for daily counts.
- **My clients** — a table of your accounts with their primary contact and status.
- **Recent meetings** — your latest scheduled calls.

If no clients are assigned yet, you'll see *"No clients assigned to you yet. Ask a super admin
to assign you via the Assignments page."*

---

## 5. My Clients & VTs — your book of business

**Open:** **My Clients & VTs** in the menu. This is your command center.

**The stats bar** shows **Clients managed, Active VTs,** and a **Productivity reports →**
shortcut.

**Each client appears as a card** with the company name, status, VT count, the primary
contact (with a clickable email), and the **Team** — each VT with a **Message** button.
Two more buttons sit on the card: **Reports** (jump to Productivity) and **Message client**.

### Approving or declining VT requests

When a client asks for another teammate, the request lands here under **VT Requests** with a
*"X to review"* badge.

1. Read the request — who asked, and which VT they want.
2. Optionally type a **note to the client** (e.g. why, or next steps).
3. Click **Approve** or **Reject**. The client is notified, and your note travels with the
   decision.
4. To tidy your view later, use **Remove from list** — the client keeps their own copy.

---

## 6. VT Assignments — managing the work

**Open:** **VT Assignments** in the menu.

Create and track tasks for the VTs on your accounts. The stats strip shows **Total, Active,
Overdue, Due today,** and **Completed**, and you can switch between **List, Month,** and
**Year** views.

**To create a task**

1. Click **New task**.
2. Optionally choose the **Client** the task belongs to.
3. Enter a **Title** (required) and a **Description** with context or links.
4. **Assign to** one of the VTs on your clients (or leave Unassigned).
5. Set the **Priority** (Low/Normal/High/Urgent) and a **Due date**.
6. Click **Create task** — the assignee is notified.

**Attachments.** Open a task and use the **Attachments** section to upload files (PDF, Office
docs, CSV, TXT, ZIP, images; up to 20 MB each).

**Complete / re-open** with the checkbox on a task's row; **edit** and **delete** with the
pencil and trash icons.

---

## 7. Meetings

**Open:** **Meetings** in the menu.

The list shows each meeting's time, client, attendees, topic, join link, and status.

**To schedule a meeting**

1. Click **New meeting**.
2. Enter the **Meeting name**.
3. Choose the **Client account** (if you manage several; if you have one, it's pre-filled).
4. Add a **Meeting link** and pick the **Call app** (Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, Webex, Phone,
   Other).
5. Set the **Day**, **Starts**, and optional **Ends** time.
6. Add **Notes** for the agenda.
7. Under **Invite teammates**, check the client contacts and VTs to invite (filter by name,
   email, or role).
8. Click **Save meeting**.

When editing, you can set the status to **Scheduled, Completed,** or **Cancelled**.

---

## 8. Productivity Reports

**Open:** **Productivity Reports** in the menu.

Monitor output across your whole book. The summary strip shows VTs, live trackers, EOD report
count, and the latest report date. Two tabs:

- **Workday Tracker** — your clients shown as cards; open one to see that client's VTs, each
  with a **Workday Tracker** button (their live time tracker) and an **EOD Reports** button.
- **EOD Reports** — every VT's daily report: the date, KPI (target vs. achieved), best work,
  help needed, and next focus. Filter to one teammate with their **EOD Reports** button;
  **Show all** clears it.

---

## 9. Messages

**Open:** **Messages** in the menu.

One-to-one chat with the clients and VTs on your accounts. Pick a contact on the left, type at
the bottom, and **Send** (or press **Enter**; **Shift+Enter** for a newline). New messages
arrive live and play a sound — mute with the bell icon. Opening a thread marks it read.

The **Message** buttons on your client cards and the **Message client** button jump you
straight into the right conversation.

---

## 10. Special Links

**Open:** **Special Links** in the menu.

Generate a secure, expiring link that opens a VT's profile with **no login required** — ideal
for sharing a candidate with a prospect or stakeholder.

1. Under **Generate a link**, choose a **Virtual Teammate**.
2. Pick a **Link lifetime** — 24 hours, 3 days, 1 week, or 30 days.
3. Click **Generate link**.
4. Your links appear on the right with a status (**Active, Expired, Revoked**) and an expiry
   countdown. Use **Copy** to grab the URL, **Open** to preview, or **Revoke** to kill a link
   immediately.

---

## 11. Resources & Knowledge Center

**Open:** **Resources** in the menu.

Your reference hub: the **Help Center** (searchable, with **Operations / Admin / Support**
filters), the **Client Playbook**, and **Client Downloads**. **Go to Knowledge Center** opens
the full structured library of guides, SOPs, and training.

---

## 12. Notifications & staying in the loop

**Open:** **Notifications** in the menu (or the top-bar bell).

Your activity inbox — VT requests, task updates, meetings, and messages, each linking to its
page.

- **Open**, **Mark as read**, or **Delete** individual items; **Mark all read** and **Delete
  all** sit at the top.
- **Email notifications** — toggle on to receive an email copy of every alert.
- **Push on this device** — after installing the app, toggle on for push notifications.

---

## 13. My Profile

**Open:** **My Profile** in the menu.

Update your **name, phone,** and **country** and click **Save profile** (email and role are
fixed). Add a **profile photo** and **cover photo** (JPG/PNG/GIF/WebP, up to 8 MB), or paste a
URL. Change your **password** on the right.

---

## 14. Signing out & quick help

- **Log out** from the bottom of the menu — essential on shared machines.
- **Password reset:** ask a super admin to reset it from the Users page.
- **Need an account assigned or a VT placed?** That's a super admin action (Assignments page) —
  flag it to operations.

---

### Quick reference — common CSM tasks

| I want to… | Go to | Then |
|------------|-------|------|
| Approve/decline a VT request | My Clients & VTs | VT Requests → Approve / Reject |
| See a client's team | My Clients & VTs | Open the client card |
| Assign a task to a VT | VT Assignments | New task → assign → Create task |
| Schedule a call | Meetings | New meeting → invite → Save meeting |
| Check team output | Productivity Reports | Workday Tracker / EOD Reports tabs |
| Share a VT profile externally | Special Links | Choose VT → lifetime → Generate link |
| Message a client or VT | Messages | Pick the person → type → Send |
| Change my password | My Profile | Change password → Update password |
